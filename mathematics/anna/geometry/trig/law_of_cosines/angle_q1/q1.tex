\begin{center}
We need to use the law of cosines.
\begin{align}
16^2 = 9^2 + 12^2 - 2*9*12*cos(C) \\
256 - (81 + 144) \rightarrow 31 \\
-\frac{31}{216} = cos(C) \\
C = cos^{-1}(-\frac{31}{216}) \approx 81.75^{\circ}
\end{align}
