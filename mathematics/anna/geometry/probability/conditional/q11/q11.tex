The probability that ten students have different birthdays is:
  \begin{align}
    P = \frac{364 * 363 * 362 * * 361 * 360 * * 359 * 358 * 357 * 356}{365} \\
    P \approx 0.883
  \end{align}
The probability that two students among the ten students share the same birthday is :
  \begin{align}
    P = 1 - \frac{364}{365} \\
    P \approx 0.002 
  \end{align}
\end{center}
