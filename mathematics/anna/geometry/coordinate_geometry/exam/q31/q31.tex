The steps for calculating the midpoint of $(-3, -6)$ and $(2, 5)$ is shown below.
\begin{align}
M = (\frac{x_1 + x_2}{2}, \frac{y_1 + y_2}{2}) \\
M = (\frac{-3 + 2}{2}, \frac{-6 + 5}{2}) \\
M = (\frac{-1}{2}, \frac{-1}{2}) 
\end{align}
