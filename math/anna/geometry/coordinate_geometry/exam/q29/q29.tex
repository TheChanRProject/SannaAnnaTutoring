The following steps show how to find the distance between $(-6, 7)$ and $(-1, -5)$.
\begin{align}
  d = \sqrt{(x_2 - x_1)^2 + (y_2 - y_1)^2} \\
  d = \sqrt{(-1 - (-6))^2 + (-5 - 7)^2} \\
  d = \sqrt{(5)^2 + (-12)^2} \rightarrow \sqrt{25 + 144} = 13 \\ 
\end{align}
